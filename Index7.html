26 April 2024: Hard to say goodbye

On Friday, we went back to our old school. We learned a new traditional dance, but this time we also taught our friends a traditional dance called the hora. It was a really special moment, and we all forgot that the hardest part of the trip was coming. It was hard for all of us, but the people there were very welcoming and hospitable.The children were as curious about us and our country as we were about them and their country.

Before bidding farewell to our friends in Poland, we visited the Fire Education Centre in Dobczyce, where we learned about the various dangers we face in life in a fun and interactive digital way. Here, we discovered the crucial skills of preventing fires and what to do in different situations, not just at home. At the end of the tour, we had the opportunity to put ourselves in the shoes of a firefighter and simulate a firefighters' route as they are called to do their duty.
